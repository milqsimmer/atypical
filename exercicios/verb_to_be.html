Verb To Be Exercises

1. Complete as frases usando o verbo "to be"

a. I a teacher.

b. You my friend.

c. They students.

d. He at home.

e. She a doctor.

2. Complete as frases usando a forma negativa do verbo "to be"

a. I a teacher.

b. You my friend.

c. They students.

d. He at home.

e. She a doctor.

3. Coloque as frases na forma interrogativa usando o verbo "to be"

a. You are a teacher.

b. He is your friend.

c. They are students.

d. She is at home.

e. It is a car.
