Present Continuous Exercises

1. Complete as frases usando o "Present Continuous Tense"

a. She (write) a letter now.

b. We (eat) dinner at the moment.

c. They (run) in the park.

d. He (talk) on the phone.

e. I (study) for my exam.

f. She (study) for her exam right now.

g. We (watch) a movie at the moment.

h. They (play) soccer in the park.

i. I (read) an interesting book these days.

j. The kids (run) in the backyard.

k. He (cook) dinner for the family tonight.

l. You (work) late today?

m. She (not/sing) in the concert this weekend.

n. We (have) lunch together at the new restaurant.

o. It (rain) a lot this week.

2. Transforme as frases no Present Continuous

a. She eats dinner. → She (eat) dinner.

b. They don't play football. → They (not/play) football.

c. I read a book. → I (read) a book.

d. We travel to London. → We (travel) to London.

e. He watches TV. → He (watch) TV.

f. The cat sleeps on the sofa. → The cat (sleep) on the sofa.

g. You don't talk to me. → You (not/talk) to me.

h. They write a letter. → They (write) a letter.

i. We walk to the park. → We (walk) to the park.

j. She cooks dinner for the family. → She (cook) dinner for the family.
